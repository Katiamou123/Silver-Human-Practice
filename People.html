🧭 How We Work

Rhythm: Weekly sprints, transparent tasks, and safety-first reviews ensure our design and data stay aligned.

Our workflow mirrors our Safety page principles: clarity, responsibility, and iteration. Each sprint pairs wet lab tasks with modeling checkpoints, and outreach planning sits alongside documentation. This keeps our science rigorous and our story coherent.

  • Design → Test → Learn: Small loops with clear criteria of success.
  • Safety built-in: BSL-1 procedures, zero environmental release in all plans.
  • Open communication: Daily syncs during experiments; async updates when modeling runs.
